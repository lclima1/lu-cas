01 / profile

My work moves across three connected practices: performing, teaching, and writing about music. As a performer, I am interested in sound, form, intensity, and the historical weight carried by musical materials. As an educator, I work with students as developing musicians rather than passive consumers of technique. As a musicologist, I investigate how music registers broader transformations in society, economy, and thought.

Performer

Classical guitarist trained at the Mozarteum in Salzburg with Marco Tamayo, with further artistic influence from major guitarists including Fabio Zanon and Elena Casoli.

04 / app

Triad Analysis App

This tool analyses MusicXML files and identifies complete triadic formations under deliberately restricted conditions. It focuses on explicitly simultaneous note events rather than reconstructing functional harmony, voice-leading, or implied tonal relations. The app was developed to support the article on Schoenberg, Adorno, and triadic presence by providing a reproducible way to quantify how often triads appear as articulated events in the score.
