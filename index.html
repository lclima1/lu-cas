01 / profile

My work moves across three connected practices: performing, teaching, and writing about music. As a performer, I am interested in sound, form, intensity, and the historical weight carried by musical materials. As an educator, I work with students as developing musicians rather than passive consumers of technique. As a musicologist, I investigate how musical structures register broader transformations in society, economy, and thought.
